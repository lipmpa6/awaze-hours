How to read this analysis. These charts show the distribution of price differences between Beyond's prices and Awaze's current prices — bucketed by percentage band. Each bar represents the share of listing-weeks falling into that bracket. The goal is not for every listing to be priced identically to today's Awaze prices; it is for the overall portfolio to be priced more accurately, more dynamically, and in a way that captures more revenue across the booking curve.

A spread across buckets is expected and intentional. Beyond's pricing model uses demand signals, booking pace, lead time, and seasonality to move prices within a defined range around the midline. That range — the corridor — is designed to give flexibility without deviating excessively from the midline anchor.

Tier deep-dive — owner level
Where owners cluster within the flat and small-drop tiers
The flat tier is genuinely centred. Of the 6,541 owners in the ±10% band, 56.5% sit within ±5% of their current midline — more than half are well inside the flat zone. Only 10.4% are in the +5% to +10% range. The largest single cluster is the −5% to −4% band (502 owners), reflecting the portfolio-level median, not a tail phenomenon.
Small-drop tier is front-weighted. Of the 2,435 owners seeing a 10–20% drop, 29% are in the −10% to −12% range — closest to the flat boundary — and 65% are between −10% and −15%. Only 11% are in the −18% to −20% range closest to the moderate-drop boundary. The average owner in this tier is at around −13%, not −18%.
